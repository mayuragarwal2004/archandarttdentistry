    About Dr. Archana Gandhi

    Meet Dr. Archana Gandhi

    Founder & Chief Dental Surgeon of Arch & Artt Dentistry

    With 23+ years of dedication, Dr. Archana Gandhi is a visionary in dental care. A Pravara Dental College (2000) graduate, she built her career from the ground up in Pune, driven by passion and determination.

    A self-made, patient-focused professional, Dr. Gandhi has successfully completed 2400+ cases, excelling in diagnostics and personalized treatment. She specializes in:

    • • Pediatric & Adult Dental Care
    • • Root Canal Treatments
    • • Extractions
    • • Digital Smile Designing
    • • Implants & Fixed Teeth
    • • Full Mouth Rehabilitation

    Holding a Fellowship in Implantology (2008), she integrates cutting-edge advancements into her practice. She is an active member of IDA, ICOI, Association of Geriatric Dentistry, JITO Women’s Wing, and IHO.

    Dr. Gandhi’s holistic approach ensures not just treatment but enhanced well-being, making her a trusted name in dentistry—transforming smiles and lives.
