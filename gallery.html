    Sterilization & Hygiene

    At The Arch and Artt Dentistry, we uphold the highest standards of sterilization and infection control to ensure a safe and hygienic environment for our patients. Our advanced protocols guarantee a germ-free and worry-free experience.

    • ✔️ Autoclave Sterilization: High-pressure steam sterilization of all dental instruments with class B autoclave.
    • ✔️ Distilled Water Plant: Ensuring the purest water for sterilization processes and patient care.

    Your health and comfort are our top priorities. Experience safe, high-quality dental care in a worry-free environment.

    State-of-the-Art Equipment

    Experience world-class dental care with our cutting-edge technology and precision instruments. Designed for comfort, safety, and accuracy, our equipment ensures a seamless and pain-free experience.

    Operatory 1

    Our Operatory 1 is equipped with the latest in dental technology, including advanced imaging systems, ergonomic patient chairs, and precision instruments to ensure maximum comfort and accuracy.

    • ✔️ Digital X-rays (RVG) for precise diagnosis
    • ✔️ Intraoral Scanner for digital impressions
    • ✔️ Ergonomic dental chairs for patient comfort
    • ✔️ LED surgical lighting for enhanced visibility

    Intraoral Scanner

    Our clinic proudly owns an advanced Intraoral Scanner from a leading global brand, offering fast, accurate, and comfortable digital impressions—enhancing treatment planning and patient experience.

    Operatory 2

    Our Operatory 2 is specially designed for surgical and specialized procedures, featuring sterilization excellence and precision tools for top-tier care.

    • ✔️ Physiodispenser for implant placement (in-house)
    • ✔️ Comprehensive Implant Kits available in-clinic
    • ✔️ HEPA air filtration for a sterile environment
    • ✔️ Laser technology for painless treatments
    • ✔️ Digital treatment planning for accuracy

    Renowned, In-House Equipment

    All advanced equipment, including our RVG systems, Intraoral Scanner, Physiodispenser, and Implant Kits, are clinic-owned, maintained in-house, and sourced from globally renowned brands—ensuring the highest standards of quality and safety.
